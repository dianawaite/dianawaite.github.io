Scooters

We have several different models of scooters available that are sure to be the perfect fit for your plans. We have small, one-person scooters, or a bit larger 2-person models that will give you a little more umph on the roads.

Honda Pioneer ATV

If you have children with you, or just want something different from the scooters, we have this Honda Pioneer ATV available that seats 4 comfortably. It is easy to drive and will get you to all the places you want to explore on the island.

Jeeps

For those who want a little more comfort while they are exploring the island, we have 2 different models of Jeeps available for rent. If you have 4-people, our 2-door Jeep is perfect for you. If your group is a little bigger, we have the 5-seat 4-door Jeep available.

  • A valid drivers license is required in hand at time of rental.
  • No one under 12 years of age are allowed to ride on the scooters.
  • Fuel charge not included.
  • Unlimited mileage.
  • Liability insurance is free of charge.
  • Reservations rate requires 24 hour prior notification.
  • Free delivery to Cozumel hotel, Airbnb, or private residence for 2+ day rentals.
  • Payment via Google Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or PayPal.
  • Rentals may be returned to either location.
